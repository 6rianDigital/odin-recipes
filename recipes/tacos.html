Tacos

Description

A quick and easy home made meal that can easily be served with a variety of options. Make your own seasoning and serve it to family and friends buffet style where people can choose the toppings they want.

Packed with protein, B-vitamins and an array of micro nutrients, home made tacos can be a healthier option. You decide how much salt and additives are used as well as the toppings. Hint: substitute greek yogurt for sour cream for a little extra protein along with probiotics and calcium.

Ingredients

  • ground beef
  • pepper
  • garlic
  • cumin
  • avacado oil
  • butter
  • tomato sauce
  • onion powder
  • salt
  • chili powder
  • corn tortillas
