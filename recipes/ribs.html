Really Good Ribs

Description

A taste of summer, grilled ribs make a great addition to any outdoor gathering. Simple and delicious, this home made marinade is sure to have everyone lining up for seconds.

Ingredients

  • 4 lbs pork spare ribs
  • 1/2 cup maple syrup
  • 1/2 cup chile sauce
  • 1/4 cup ketchup
  • 1/4 cup soy sauce
  • 1/4 cup worcestershire sauce
  • 1/4 cup rum
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tsp dry mustard
  • 1 dash ground black pepper

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350 F
  2. Cut spare ribs into serving size portions, wrap in double thick tin foil.
  3. Bake in preheated oven for 1.5 hours. Unwrap and drain drippings, place in large roasting pan.
  4. Mix together maple syrup, chile, ketchup, soy, worcestershire, rum, garlic, mustard and pepper in a bowl. Coat ribs with sauce and marinate at room temperature for 1 hour or in refrigerator overnight.
  5. Preheat grill to medium heat. Cook ribs on preheated grill for 30 minutes, basting with marinade.
